About me

Hi, I'm Zainab — a frontend developer who finds her sweet spot right where design meets code. I'm currently studying HBO Media, and what drives me is the idea that a website should never just function — it should feel good to use. Every hover effect, every smooth transition, every carefully chosen color has a purpose. I have a passion for clean, intentional design and I love the challenge of translating a visual idea into working, polished code. Whether it's building a layout from scratch, fine-tuning animations or making sure everything looks just right on every screen — I care about the details that most people don't even consciously notice, but always feel. When I'm not coding, I find inspiration in typography, visual art and anything with a strong aesthetic. I'm always learning, always experimenting, and always looking for ways to make the web a little more beautiful.
